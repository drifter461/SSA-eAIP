## Phraseology

### Standard Departure (SID)
!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), Routing xxx, FLxxx, Departure Runway XX, SID, Transition X, climb to FL090."

    Example: "Mozambique 190, cleared to Nampula, Routing UW19, FL350, Departure Runway 05, UVAP1A, Transition IMTAG, climb to FL090."

!!! tip
    Omit the Transition if not required

### Non-standard Departure
!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), Routing xxx, FLxxx, Departure Runway XX, maintain runway track/Right Turnout/Left Turnout, climb to FL090."

    Example: "Mozambique 126, cleared to Inhambane, Routing UW15, FL210, Departure Runway 05, Right Turnout, climb to FL090."
