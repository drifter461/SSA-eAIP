# Delivery

!!! info
    Victoria Falls does not have a dedicated Delivery frequency, as such all Delivery responsibilities are assumed by Falls Approach (FVFA_APP). Under no circumstances should a FVFA_DEL position ever be opened without prior approval from staff.

## Departure Procedures

!!! info
    There are no Standard Instrument Departures out of Victoria Falls. All departures are to be told to maintain runwway track.

## Phraseology

!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), after departure Runway XX, non standard, maintain runway track, climb to FL090, squawk (squawk)"
