# Pilot Clients

Pilot clients are essential for pilots to connect to the network. The most popular ones are vPilot for VATSIM and xPilot for X-plane. Whilst they are for different simulators, they function relatively the same.

## Download links
- [vPilot](https://vpilot.rosscarlson.dev/Download)
- [xPilot](https://github.com/xpilot-project/xpilot/releases/tag/v3.0.2)
